Everything you need to know about our community-driven premium model

Premium access is earned through meaningful community engagement. This includes commenting thoughtfully on stories, participating in discussions, sharing stories you love, voting on community content, and contributing to our writing challenges. As you reach specific engagement milestones, premium features are automatically unlocked for your account.

We value quality over quantity. Meaningful engagement includes detailed, constructive comments on stories, helpful feedback to authors, participating in community discussions, sharing stories with thoughtful recommendations, and contributing original content. Simple "great story!" comments won't count as much as detailed, helpful feedback that shows you've genuinely engaged with the content.

The timeline varies based on your level of engagement. Active community members typically unlock their first premium features within 2-4 weeks of consistent participation. Full premium access usually takes 2-3 months of regular, meaningful engagement.
